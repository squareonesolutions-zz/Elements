SPA TREATMENTS MENU

Elements Spa Hawaii is an Aveda family day spa.  All our service providers are licensed estheticians and/or massage therapists in the State of Hawaii and receive regular training to stay abreast of the latest trends in skincare and massage.  We use only quality Aveda, Biotone and Sircuit Cosmeceuticals products in our massages and facials.
