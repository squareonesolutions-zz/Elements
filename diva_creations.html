"Diva Creations" Stylist Peaches Howard:

At the age of 16, Peaches Howard realized that hairstyling might play a huge role in her future.  Her older sister had been styling for years and it showed Peaches the kind of lifestyle that she could see herself living. Her first college experience was directing her towards teaching the special needs and the deaf, but after changing focuses and enrolling into a beauty school, she started in the salon industry in 1984. 

Peaches is a very family oriented person, which is what brought her to Hawaii, and in turn, here to Elements Spa & Salon. Known as Elements’ "DIVA CREATIONS" master stylist, she brings years of experience and certifications to the salon, though she is just now embracing the family of Aveda, which she loves the more she uses it. 

She specializes in all different kinds of styling from hair extensions and track weave, to perms, relaxers and the Global Keratin Hair Taming System (similar to the Brazilian Blowout). To Peaches, there is no favorite style; the best day is one with a variety of services.
