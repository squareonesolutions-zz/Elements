THE DESTINATION - For Your Ultimate Spa Experience


Entrepreneur Chi Kanu purchased Elements Spa and Salon in July 2008. Chi brings years of successful business experience into the mix. Her love and enjoyment of the spa environment, Hawaiian weather and beauty, prompted her to purchase Elements and relocate to Hawaii.

Chi’s vision for Elements is to provide an upscale and elegant place with exceptional spa experience to each guest. Elements is an intimate spa and salon where every guest can unwind, relax and take a vacation from their day-to-day activities.

Her commitment to excellence in customer service and selective product offerings are the key to driving the growth of Elements. Chi will continue to focus on providing a place that is welcoming for relaxation and pampering while taking care of the guests’ health and wellness. Whether you come to Elements for a massage, facial, hair, nail or wax services, your experience at Elements will be something special.

"Elements" is the spa with unique services where you spend the day among friends. Make Elements your destination for the ultimate spa experience.
