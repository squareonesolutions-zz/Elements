Elements Spa Hawaii is an Aveda family day spa.  All our service providers are licensed estheticians and/or massage therapists in the State of Hawaii and receive regular training to stay abreast of the latest trends in skincare and massage.  We use only quality Aveda, Biotone and Sircuit Cosmeceuticals products in our massages and facials.

Our massage therapists and estheticians are trained in a variety of massage and skincare techniques to achieve the maximum results for each client.  All massages and facials are customized to meet the specific needs of each client.

We at Elements Spa and Salon Hawaii believe that each and every client is a guest and should receive the full spa experience.  Every spa treatment at Elements Hawaii begins with a relaxing tea service and a consultation so that our therapists are able to customize the treatment to address individual clients’ needs, concerns and expectations.  In addition, all one-hour spa services include either a complimentary mini facial or foot treatment, and a complimentary makeup touch-up upon request when booking.
 

CONFIRMATIONS AND CANCELLATIONS

All spa and salon appointments will be confirmed with each guest the day before their spa experience. As a courtesy to our service providers as well as other guests please reschedule or cancel any appointment at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation charge. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice will be subject to the full charge of the treatment. All Spa and Salon services and packages must be held with a credit card.
