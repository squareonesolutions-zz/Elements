DRIVING DIRECTIONS

           From Waikiki (approx. 5 min)

  • Take Ala Wai Boulevard (the street along the canal – one way)
  • Turn right on Kalakaua Avenue  (almost at the end of Ala Wai)
  • Cross the bridge and get into the middle lane (the Convention Center will be on your left)
  • Turn left on Kapiolani Boulevard
  • Take your next immediate right onto Kalauokalani Way
  • Park on Kalauokalani Way in the large empty lot on your right in a stall that is yellow on both sides.

           From Ala Moana (approx. 2 min)

  • Exit Ala Moana onto Keeaumoku Street (exit near Nordstrom)
  • Cross through Kapiolani Boulevard and make your next right onto Makaloa Street (by Walgreens)
  • Keep going down Makaloa Avenue and pass First Hawaiian Bank on your right and Blockbuster on your right.
  • Turn right onto Kalauokalani Way (immediately after passing Blockbuster on your right).
  • Park on Kalauokalani Way in the large empty lot on your left (at the end of the street) in a stall that is yellow on both sides.

           From University of Hawaii (approx. 10 min)

  • Go down University Avenue (toward Waikiki)
  • Turn right on Kapiolani Boulevard
  • Keep going down Kapiolani Boulevard through several traffic lights.  PassMcCullyShopping Center on your right.
  • Go through Kalakaua Avenue (pass Hard Rock on your left)
  • Take your next immediate right onto Kalauokalani Way.
  • Park on Kalauokalani Way in the large empty lot on your right in a stall that is yellow on both sides.

           From Downtown (approx. 10 min)

  • Take King Street all the way to the Ala Moana area (pass McKinleyHigh School and Keeaumoku Street)
  • Turn right on Kalakaua Avenue (Jack In the Box on the corner)
  • Pass through several traffic lights
  • Turn right on Kapiolani Boulevard (Convention Center on the corner)
  • Take your next immediate right onto Kalauokalani Way.
  • Park on Kalauokalani Way in the large empty lot on your right in a stall that is yellow on both sides.

           From H-1 East

  • Take the Punahou Exit (after Ala Moana)
  • Turn right after getting off the freeway.
  • Turn right at the next traffic light (Beretania Street) and get into your far left lane.
  • Immediately turn left onto Kalakaua Avenue.
  • Pass through several traffic lights
  • Turn right on Kapiolani Boulevard (Convention Center on the corner)
  • Take your next immediate right onto Kalauokalani Way.
  • Park on Kalauokalani Way in the large empty lot on your right in a stall that is yellow on both sides.

         From H-1 West 

  • Take the Kapiolani Boulevard Exit
  • Keep going down Kapiolani Boulevard through several traffic lights.  PassMcCullyShopping Center on your right.
  • Go through Kalakaua Avenue (pass Hard Rock on your left)
  • Take your next immediate right onto Kalauokalani Way.
  • Park on Kalauokalani Way in the large empty lot on your right in a stall that is yellow on both sides.
