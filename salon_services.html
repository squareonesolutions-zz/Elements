Elements Salon Hawaii is an Aveda family beauty salon. All our stylists are licensed in the State of Hawaii and receive regular training from Aveda professionals to stay abreast of the latest trends in style, cuts and color. Elements Salon Hawaii is a high-end beauty salon and uses only quality Aveda color as its exclusive hair color line and Aveda hair care and styling products.

All-Inclusive Service
Elements Salon Hawaii includes the full salon and spa experience with every service.  Thus, unless noted otherwise, all haircut and color prices include complimentary treatments, a consultation, shampoo and conditioning with quality Aveda products, and a blowdry and style. 

Complimentary Treatments
We at Elements Salon Hawaii believe that salon clients should receive a spa experience as well.  Thus, all salon services at Elements come with pampering extras like tea service, a scalp massage, a mini facial at the shampoo bowl, a hand massage, and a free two-week clean-up for men or a free bang-trim for women between haircuts.

Exclusive Aveda Color Line
Elements Salon Hawaii uses Aveda hair color as its exclusive color line.  Aveda uses only natural ingredients and its color line is 97% naturally derived.  All color services are customized and administered by a color specialist trained in Aveda color to achieve the right color and application for our clients.

Style
Try something new for your next special event with an updo, perm, or real hair extensions.  At Elements Salon Hawaii we use only all-natural real hair extensions that can be styled, colored, and cut like your real hair. 

   

CONFIRMATIONS AND CANCELLATIONS

All spa and salon appointments will be confirmed with each guest the day before their spa experience. As a courtesy to our service providers as well as other guests please reschedule or cancel any appointment at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation charge. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice will be subject to the full charge of the treatment. All Spa and Salon services and packages must be held with a credit card.
