Elements Spa and Salon Hawaii is an intimate hair salon and day spa located in the heart of Honolulu, Hawaii.  At Elements Spa and Salon Hawaii, you will be treated like a friend each and every time you visit our salon.  It’s not just the quality products and services that we strive to provide, it’s the added complimentary treatments, the familiar faces and the intimate atmosphere that make Elements Spa and Salon the premiere beauty salon in Honolulu. 

Located just across the street from the Hawaii Convention Center, Elements Spa and Salon Hawaii is the perfect place for a quick retreat, a place for your regular maintenance services, or an elegant start to your wedding day or special event.  Schedule a couples’ treatment with a loved one or experience one of our all day spa packages.  Our service menu is comprehensive so we can accommodate all your beauty needs in one place.  Service providers at Elements are fully licensed and trained by Aveda professionals so you can count on us to keep you looking and feeling great all year.   

At Elements Hawaii, we believe that every client is a friend and every treatment should be a spa experience.  Thus, we include little extras like tea service, foot treatments, mini facials, and scalp massages with our full salon and spa treatments.   We are a salon for Hawaii residents and visitors alike so our services are priced at rates for all to enjoy.  We invite you to experience Elements and spend the day among friends.
